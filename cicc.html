About CICC Club

Tradition since 1889

CINEMATOGRAPHY OF INNOVATION AND CREATIVITY CLUB was founded on August 7th, 2015 as a domain of Arts Club. After two successful years, it has become an Official Photography Club of SREENIDHI INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY on August-7-2017 under the guidance of our beloved Executive Director Dr.P Narasimha Reddy and Faculty head, Dr.Venkat Reddy.

Photography is the key part of Sreenidhi photography club because it is the unique department in which it has the power to capture memories through the lens. We portray a picturesque perspective of the college. Sreenidhi photography club is to encourage student's interests to capture the beauty and elegance of the world through photography.
